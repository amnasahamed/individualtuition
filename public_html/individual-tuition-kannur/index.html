Individual Tuition in Kannur

North Kerala students in Kannur get the same expert one-to-one online tuition as metro cities — Kerala State syllabus, CBSE, and board exam preparation.

CLAPS provides individual online tuition to Kannur and north Kerala families with flexible scheduling and Malayalam-friendly teaching options.

Quality tuition without leaving Kannur

Geography should not limit access to great teachers. CLAPS connects Kannur students with subject experts online for private sessions tailored to SSLC, Plus Two, or primary-level gaps.

How CLAPS individual tuition works

Every class is one teacher and one student on live video. You message WhatsApp, share your child's class and syllabus, and we assign a subject expert. Coordinators remain available 10 AM – 10 PM for scheduling and progress questions.

Syllabuses we teach

Kerala State (SCERT) — primary through Plus Two including SSLC preparation.
CBSE / NCERT — Class 1 to 12 with board exam focus.
ICSE / ISC — detailed syllabus coverage including literature-heavy papers.
Malayalam-medium or bilingual explanation available when helpful.

Subjects available

Maths, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English, Malayalam, Hindi, Social Science, Accountancy, Economics, Business Studies, Computer Science, and all standard school subjects from KG upward.

Common questions

Is CLAPS online or home visit?

CLAPS is fully online — live one-to-one video classes. No tutor travels to your home, which saves time and expands teacher choice.

Which syllabuses do you teach?

Kerala State (SCERT), CBSE, ICSE, and ISC from KG through Plus Two (+2).

Can I choose only one subject?

Yes. Pick any subjects — for example Maths only, or Class 10 Science only.

What are class timings?

Flexible — typically after school (4 PM–9 PM IST) and weekends. NRI families get Gulf-friendly slots too.
