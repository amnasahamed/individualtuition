Getting started

What is individual tuition?

Individual tuition (also called one-to-one tuition) means one dedicated teacher teaches one student in a private session. At CLAPS, every class is individual — we never run group batches. The teacher adapts pace, language, and focus to your child for the entire session, so weak chapters get the time they need.

Is CLAPS the same as ClapsLearn?

CLAPS Individual Tuition is a focused sub-brand of ClapsLearn (clapslearn.com). ClapsLearn is the parent online education platform serving 10,000+ students; individualtuition.in is dedicated to one-to-one tuition enquiries and enrolment.

Who is CLAPS best for?

Families who want personal attention for their child — students falling behind in one or two subjects, board-exam years needing targeted revision, NRI families continuing CBSE/Kerala syllabus abroad, and parents who prefer expert teachers over local availability. CLAPS is less suited for families looking for group batch tuition — we focus exclusively on individual classes.

How do I enrol?

Message us on WhatsApp at +91 86104 16147 or use the green WhatsApp button on this site. Share your child's class, board, subjects, and a few preferred time slots. A coordinator confirms teacher availability — usually within a few hours — and your first session is scheduled.

Is there a free demo class?

Yes. We arrange a short introductory session so your child meets the assigned teacher before committing. The demo confirms fit on both sides — your child experiences the live format, and you can ask the coordinator any remaining questions before starting regular classes.

Classes & teachers

Which classes and syllabuses do you cover?

KG through Plus Two (+2). Syllabuses include CBSE (NCERT), Kerala State (SCERT), ICSE, and ISC. Other boards (Cambridge IGCSE, IB, state boards on request) can also be supported — message WhatsApp with your child's exact syllabus to confirm a teacher.

Can I choose only specific subjects?

Yes. You pick only the subjects your child needs — for example, just Class 10 Maths and Science, or Plus Two Physics alone. There is no all-subject bundle requirement. Pay only for the subjects you book.

Are all CLAPS classes truly one-to-one?

Yes — every single class is one teacher and one student on live video. We do not run group sessions, shared batches, or '1:2' pairings. The teacher is fully focused on your child for the entire session.

How are CLAPS teachers selected?

Teachers are subject-specialist graduates and post-graduates with prior online or classroom teaching experience. Each teacher goes through a multi-round demo, syllabus test, and mock-class evaluation before being assigned to students. CLAPS has 3,000+ vetted teachers on the platform.

Can I switch teachers if it isn't a good fit?

Yes. Tell your coordinator — we re-match your child with another teacher at no extra cost. Compatibility matters more than continuity, especially for young learners, so we make switching easy and judgment-free.

Do you teach in Malayalam?

Yes. Many teachers support Malayalam-medium or bilingual explanation, especially for Kerala State syllabus and primary students. For higher classes and CBSE/ICSE, sessions typically run in English with Malayalam support on request. Mention your preference at enrolment.

Schedule & sessions

Can we pick class timings?

Yes. CLAPS arranges classes at your preferred time slots where teacher availability allows — typically after school (4 PM–9 PM IST) and weekends for India families, and Gulf-friendly evening slots for NRI families. UK and US slots are scheduled based on local time zones.

How long is each session?

Most sessions are 45–60 minutes per subject. Younger students (KG–Class 3) often do better with 30–40 minutes. Frequency is typically 2–5 sessions per week per subject — you can increase before board exams or reduce during holidays.

What if my child misses a class?

Just message your coordinator in advance (WhatsApp works best). We reschedule the missed session to another slot in the same week when the teacher is free, or carry it forward. We are flexible on genuine illness, school events, and family travel.

How do reschedules work?

Reschedules are free with reasonable notice (typically 4+ hours). For last-minute cancellations, the coordinator works with you to find the next available slot. Regular patterns — for example, every Tuesday 6 PM — are scheduled in advance and stay stable week to week.

Tech & setup

Are classes fully online?

Yes. All CLAPS individual tuition classes are live online sessions with interactive teaching tools — video, audio, screen-share, and a digital writing surface. You need a device with internet — phone, tablet, or laptop. No tutor travels to your home.

What device and internet do we need?

Any smartphone, tablet, or laptop made in the last 5–6 years works fine. A headset with a mic improves audio clarity. For internet, a stable 5 Mbps connection is enough — Wi-Fi at home or a 4G mobile hotspot both work. CLAPS does not require any special software purchase.

Do you provide an app or platform?

Sessions run on a standard web-based video teaching tool — your child joins via a link or one-click access. No installation is required on phones or laptops. Parents receive class reminders and updates on WhatsApp from the coordinator.

Are sessions recorded for later viewing?

Sessions are not recorded by default — CLAPS focuses on live interaction. If your child misses a session and wants to revisit a topic, the teacher can provide a short summary, worked examples, or a written recap instead. Speak to your coordinator if you want a different arrangement.

Fees, trials & billing

What are the fees?

Fees depend on the class, subject, syllabus, and weekly frequency. CLAPS keeps pricing transparent — message us on WhatsApp with your child's details and we share the exact monthly fee for your plan before you commit. There are no hidden charges.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes — the first short introductory session is free so you can experience the format. After that, regular sessions are billed monthly. You can pause or discontinue any time with notice — there is no long-term lock-in.

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI, bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS), and card payments are supported. NRI families can pay via international cards or bank transfer in INR. Invoices and receipts are shared on WhatsApp after each payment.

Is there a sibling discount?

Yes — siblings studying together get a reduced fee per child. Tell your coordinator about both children upfront and we will work out the combined plan. Each child still gets their own teacher and session for true one-to-one benefit.

NRI & international families

Do you serve NRI students abroad?

Yes. We teach students in the Gulf (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Bahrain), UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Singapore, Malaysia, and 12+ countries. Sessions are scheduled around your local time zone and your child's school hours.

Which countries do you support?

All major NRI destinations — UAE (Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah), Saudi Arabia (Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam), Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Bahrain, UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Singapore, Malaysia, and more. Coordinator support works in IST daytime, but classes follow your local evening slots.

How do timezones work for NRI students?

Teachers are assigned based on timezone overlap. Gulf students typically attend 6 PM–10 PM local time. UK families usually schedule late afternoon UK time (4 PM–8 PM GMT/BST). US East Coast families often take early morning IST slots that match US evenings.

Results & quality

Does research support individual tuition?

Yes. Multiple meta-analyses and government evidence reviews find that one-to-one tuition produces substantial learning gains — larger than typical classroom interventions. See our research page for citations from Bloom (1984), Cohen et al. (1982), Nickow et al. (2020), the Education Endowment Foundation, and ASER-based studies in India.

Is individual tuition better than group tuition?

Research comparing one-to-one and small-group tuition is mixed on average effect sizes, but one-to-one always maximises individual attention, pacing, and doubt-clearing. CLAPS focuses exclusively on individual tuition because parents choose us specifically for that personal model — not batch classes.

What results can parents expect?

Most parents see clearer concept understanding within 4–6 weeks and visible improvement in school tests within a term. Board-exam year students typically show measurable score gains — we attribute this to consistent personal attention and the absence of 'lost' time spent waiting in batch lectures. CLAPS is rated 4.9/5 on Google from 1,000+ parent reviews.

Do you offer board exam and entrance prep?

Yes. Board exam revision tuition for Class 10 (SSLC, CBSE, ICSE) and Plus Two is a major focus at CLAPS. We also support NEET and JEE foundation alongside the school syllabus, though we don't run full-time entrance crash courses — for that, dedicated coaching institutes are more suitable.

What support do parents get?

Academic coordinators are available 10 AM to 10 PM IST daily via WhatsApp for scheduling, teacher queries, and progress follow-up. After each session, parents can request a brief update from the teacher on what was covered. For longer-term plans, coordinators review progress monthly with parents.
