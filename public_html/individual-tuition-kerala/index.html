Individual Tuition in Kerala

Trusted online individual tuition for Kerala students — Kerala State syllabus, CBSE, ICSE from KG to +2. Teachers who understand Malayalam-medium and English-medium learners.

CLAPS provides individual tuition for Kerala students online, covering Kerala State syllabus and national boards with flexible scheduling for families across Kerala and Malayali diaspora worldwide.

Individual tuition for Kerala students

Kerala has one of India's highest literacy rates — and parents expect quality, personalised learning. CLAPS Individual Tuition delivers one teacher per student online, so your child gets full attention whether they follow Kerala State syllabus or CBSE/ICSE.

Syllabuses we cover for Kerala

  • Kerala State syllabus — SCERT-aligned teaching from primary to +2
  • CBSE — popular in urban Kerala schools
  • ICSE / ISC — structured curriculum support

Malayalam & English medium

Many Kerala families prefer teachers who can explain concepts in Malayalam when needed, while building English fluency for exams. CLAPS matches students with teachers suited to their medium and comfort level.

Individual tuition across every Kerala district

CLAPS serves families in all 14 districts — from Kasaragod to Thiruvananthapuram — through online one-to-one classes. Whether you are in a city centre or a smaller town, the same expert teachers and coordinators support your child.

Kerala boards we specialise in

Kerala State syllabus (SCERT) — the most common board in government and aided schools, including SSLC and Plus Two Higher Secondary exams.
CBSE — growing in urban Kerala cities with NCERT textbook alignment.
ICSE/ISC — popular in select private schools with in-depth literature and Science.

Malayalam-medium and English-medium

Many Kerala students think more clearly when difficult concepts are explained in Malayalam first. CLAPS teachers can switch between Malayalam and English based on your preference — especially helpful for primary students and Kerala State syllabus learners.

Why Kerala leads India in supplementary tuition

Kerala's high literacy culture means parents invest seriously in education. ASER and academic research confirm that structured tuition — especially when targeted to a child's gaps — improves learning outcomes. CLAPS individual tuition applies that principle with modern online delivery.
